About

EcoVoyage is a web application dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism. It allows users to discover eco-friendly travel destinations, activities connected to nature, and environmentally certified accommodations. The platform also helps users plan their journeys using public transportation, encouraging greener travel options. EcoVoyage is designed to make responsible travel both accessible and inspiring, supporting eco-conscious choices without compromising on convenience.
