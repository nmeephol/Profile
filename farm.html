Broiler Farm Design

EXPLORATION




About

This project involved designing a UI prototype for a mobile application tailored to broiler farm management. To create a solution that truly meets user needs, the process began with on-site learning to understand the daily operations of poultry farms. This was followed by direct interviews with farmers to gain insights into their challenges and expectations. The outcome is a user-focused UI prototype designed to enhance efficiency, address specific pain points, and support the practical needs of broiler farmers.
